A better repair

Original Parts

Original parts are critical to a quality repair and to keep your device functioning correctly. At iPatch we only fit original parts where available. You can rest assured knowing you're receiving the best quality parts and service.

Maintenance

We clean all Mac's we open to remove any dust or other dirt. This keeps your Mac cool and should help maintain performance and durability. We also replace thermal compound on older machines to keep your processors cool.

Quality

It's not just our parts that are original, we aim to repair your device to the same standard as it left the factory. This means every screw, every shield plate and any seals are put back or replaced if they are missing.

Fast Turnaround

We aim to complete most Mac repairs in the same day, with or without an appointment. Some repairs do take longer to complete, such as logic board board faults and liquid damage inspections. However these shouldn't take more than a few days.

Mail in repairs are completed and return posted the same day we recieve them. This means our turnaround time is usually 48 hours.
